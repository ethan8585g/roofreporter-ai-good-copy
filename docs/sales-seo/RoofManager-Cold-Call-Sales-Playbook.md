# RoofManager.ca — Cold Call Sales Playbook

**For: Ethan (Founder)**
**Target: Roofing companies, roofing contractors, and solar sales companies**

---

## THE MINDSET

You're not selling software. You're calling a busy contractor who's drowning in manual measurements, lost leads, and wasted drive time — and you have the fix. You already know their pain better than they do because you built the solution. Be confident, be fast, be real. No corporate fluff. You're one roofing guy helping another roofing guy win.

---

## PRE-CALL PREP (2 minutes per prospect)

Before you dial, pull up the company and have these ready:

1. **Company name + owner's first name** (Google, BBB, their website "About" page)
2. **Their service area** (so you can say "roofing companies in [their city]")
3. **Something specific** — a recent Google review, a job photo on their Facebook, their website. Anything that shows you're not reading off a list.
4. **Their weak spot** — bad website? No Google reviews? Still using pen-and-paper estimates? That's your angle.

---

## THE SCRIPT

### OPENER — Warm, Fast, Familiar (first 10 seconds)

> **"Hey [First Name]! It's Ethan, how's it going man?"**

*[Pause — let them respond. They'll either say "good" or "who's this?"]*

**If they say "Good, who's this?":**

> **"Ethan from Roof Manager — I'll be super quick with you, I know you're busy. Do you have 60 seconds?"**

**If they say "Yeah, good" (playing along):**

> **"Awesome. Hey listen, I'll be real quick — I know you've got jobs to run."**

---

### THE HOOK — Simple, Direct, No BS (next 15 seconds)

> **"So the reason I'm calling — I work with roofing companies in [their area] and we've built a platform that's saving guys a serious amount of time and money on their measurement reports, estimates, and lead management. I'm not trying to sell you anything on this call, I just want to show you what we're doing because I think you'd get a lot out of it."**

---

### THE VALUE DROP — Stack the Benefits Fast (next 30 seconds)

> **"Real quick — what we do is, you punch in any address, and within minutes you get a full professional roof measurement report. We're talking square footage, pitch, ridges, hips, valleys, edges — everything. All satellite and AI-powered. No climbing on roofs, no drones, no waiting two days for an EagleView report."**

*[Pause for reaction]*

> **"On top of that, the whole platform is a CRM built specifically for roofing and solar companies. So your leads, your pipeline, your invoicing, your customer follow-ups, your proposals — it's all in one place. Most guys I talk to are running QuickBooks over here, some spreadsheet over there, sticky notes on the dash — we eliminate all of that."**

---

### THE DIFFERENTIATOR — Why You, Why Now

Use whichever of these hits hardest based on what you know about them:

**For the guy still using EagleView / RoofScope:**
> **"What are you paying for measurement reports right now? Most guys are dropping $30-50 a pop on EagleView and waiting hours or days. We do it faster, and the reports are just as detailed — plus you get the full CRM, pipeline management, and AI analysis on top of it."**

**For the guy with no system:**
> **"Let me ask you this — when a lead comes in right now, what's your process? Because most guys I talk to, they're writing it on a napkin, maybe texting their sales guy, and half those leads just fall through the cracks. We fix that completely."**

**For the solar company:**
> **"We also have a full solar design builder and solar report system built in. So if you're doing solar alongside roofing, you can run both sides of the business from one platform — measurements, designs, proposals, the whole thing."**

---

### THE CLOSE — Book the Demo, Nothing Else

> **"Look, I'm not asking you to buy anything today. What I'd love to do is just jump on a quick 15-minute screen share, show you the platform, and you tell me if it makes sense for your business. If it does, great. If not, no hard feelings at all. What does your [tomorrow/Thursday/this week] look like?"**

**If they push back "Just send me some info":**
> **"Totally — I'll shoot you over some stuff. But honestly man, this is one of those things that makes way more sense when you see it live. It'll take 15 minutes and I promise I won't waste your time. What about [specific day and time]?"**

**If they're genuinely not interested:**
> **"No worries at all man, I appreciate your time. If you ever want to check it out, it's roofmanager.ca — we're not going anywhere. Take care brother."**

*(Be cool. They might come back in 3 months.)*

## OBJECTION HANDLING

### "What does it cost?"
> **"It depends on the plan and volume, but I'll walk you through all of that on the demo. The way most guys look at it — if it saves you even one or two hours a week and you close one extra deal a month because your leads aren't slipping through the cracks, it pays for itself ten times over. Let me just show you what it does first."**

### "We already use [competitor]"
> **"Nice, what do you like about it? [Listen.] Yeah that's solid. The difference with us is you're getting the measurements AND the full CRM and pipeline in one platform — so you're not duct-taping three different tools together. Plus our AI analysis gives you stuff nobody else is doing right now. Worth a 15-minute look at least, right?"**

### "I don't have time"
> **"I hear you — you're a roofer, not a desk jockey, I get it. That's literally why we built this. The whole point is to save you time. Give me 15 minutes this week and I'll prove it. What's better, morning or afternoon?"**

### "I need to talk to my partner/wife/business partner"
> **"Totally understand. How about this — let's do the quick demo together, and then you'll have everything you need to bring back to them. Way easier than trying to explain it secondhand. Does [day] work?"**

### "Just email me"
> **"For sure, what's your best email? [Get it.] I'll send that over today. But just so it doesn't get buried in your inbox — let's pencil in a quick call for [2 days out] so I can walk you through it live. If you've already seen enough from the email, we can cancel. Fair?"**

## KEY FEATURES TO REFERENCE ON CALLS

Keep these in your back pocket. Don't dump them all at once — drop them based on what the prospect cares about:

- **Instant satellite roof measurements** — address in, full report out. Area, pitch, segments, edges, ridges, hips, valleys.
- **AI-powered roof analysis** — Gemini vision AI inspects roof condition from imagery.
- **Professional PDF reports** — branded, client-ready, three-page reports generated automatically.
- **Full CRM and pipeline** — leads, customers, jobs, follow-ups, pipeline stages, all in one dashboard.
- **Invoicing and payments** — Stripe and Square integration built in. Send invoices, collect payments.
- **Solar design builder** — full solar panel layout tool with energy production estimates.
- **Solar reports** — Google Solar API integration for instant solar viability data.
- **AI receptionist** — voice agent that answers calls, books appointments, captures leads 24/7.
- **Lead capture widgets** — embeddable forms for their website that feed straight into the CRM.
- **Door-to-door tools** — built-in D2D canvassing features for field sales teams.
- **Email outreach** — automated email campaigns to nurture leads.
- **Team management** — add sales reps, assign territories, track performance.
- **Google Business + Google Ads integration** — manage reputation and ad spend from the platform.
- **Website builder** — spin up a contractor website right from the dashboard.
- **Calendar and scheduling** — book appointments, sync with team calendars.
- **Customer portal** — homeowners can view their reports, proposals, and invoices online.
- **Virtual try-on** — homeowners can visualize different roofing materials on their home.

## DAILY CALL TARGETS

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Dials per day | 50-80 |
| Conversations (live pickups) | 15-20 |
| Demos booked per day | 3-5 |
| Demos completed per week | 10-15 |
| Close rate goal | 20-30% of demos |

**Best calling windows for contractors:**
- **7:00 - 8:30 AM** (before they head to job sites)
- **12:00 - 1:00 PM** (lunch break)
- **4:30 - 6:00 PM** (wrapping up for the day)

Avoid calling 9-11 AM and 2-4 PM — they're on roofs.

---

## TONE REMINDERS

- **Be a peer, not a salesperson.** You built this because you understand the industry.
- **Talk fast but not frantic.** High energy, not desperate energy.
- **Use their language.** Say "guys" not "clients." Say "jobs" not "projects." Say "close deals" not "convert leads."
- **Be okay with no.** The fastest way to lose credibility is to beg. Abundance mindset — there are thousands of roofing companies.
- **Have fun with it.** If you're not enjoying the call, they won't enjoy talking to you.
